Butterfly

  • Butterflies are colorful insects that are known for their delicate wings and graceful flight.
  • They undergo a remarkable transformation from a caterpillar to a beautiful butterfly through a process called metamorphosis.
  • Butterflies play a crucial role in pollination, as they feed on nectar from flowers and inadvertently transfer pollen from one flower to another.
  • These fascinating creatures can be found in various habitats around the world, and their vibrant colors and patterns make them a popular subject of admiration and study among nature enthusiasts.
