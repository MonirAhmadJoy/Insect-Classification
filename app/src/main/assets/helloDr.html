Dragonfly

  • Dragonflies are insects belonging to the order Odonata.
  • They are characterized by their large compound eyes, long and slender bodies, and four transparent wings.
  • Dragonflies are agile fliers, capable of swift and precise movements.
  • They are often found near water bodies such as lakes, ponds, and streams, where they lay their eggs and spend the majority of their life cycle as nymphs underwater before transforming into adults.
